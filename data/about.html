About

Bill Pulse is a two-way app that informs and at the same time represents the Filipino people. With the mission to provide the people with the power of knowledge and training of how it may be used, our vision is to shape the Filipino people to becoming well-informed individuals, aware of their environment, and ready to act for the good of their communities.

About the Developers

THe team "Where is Rog?" is composed of Adrian Del Campo, Rogiella Isidro, Almond Valleza, and Paulo Perez, who are students in the University of the Philippines Diliman. Together with their mentor, Sir Mel Boza, the team is dedicated towards social awareness and engagement to the public when it comes to the state and issues in the country
